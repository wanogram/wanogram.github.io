About us

Sikhangezile & Craftsmen Ltd. is a multi-disciplinary civil engineering construction company founded in 2014 with the objective of increasing innovation in the civil engineering world. The construction industry has been around for many years however, we now live in a digital era thus creating a technological shift in an “old” industry. Our mission at Sikhangezile Estates & Craftsmen Projects is to develop, build and service Southern Africa and it’s needs for better living, working and traveling conditions whilst matching the newer technological trends.
